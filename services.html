Greenplanet Lawn Care provides general care for domestic horticulture. Greenplanet is able to provide holistic lawn care under many conditions, but did you know there are certain conditions in which it is ideal to tend to your lawn? Soil temperatures lower than 35 degrees farenheit are considerably poor for growing vegetation. Temperatures between 35 and 50 degrees are moderate. While soil temperatures ranging between 50 and 75 degrees farenheit are ideal for vegetation growth. Another key factor in the Earth's natural growing process is ground moisture, which is the volume of water in a given area of soil. The greater the saturation, the more vegetation will grow. A major contributor to both of these factors is weather, thus it is important to consider all of these factors when considering when to assess lawn care.
