About Greenplanet Landscaping

Greenplanet Landscaping Inc. was founded by Arturo Soto, and currently operates out of El Paso, TX. Arturo and his team have been in operation since 2007, wielding experience in a wide variety of jobs. The focus of services is centered around domestic lawncare and horticulture needs of all types.
